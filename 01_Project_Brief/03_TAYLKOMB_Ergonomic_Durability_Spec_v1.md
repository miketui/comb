## 1. Purpose

This document specifies the minimum ergonomic comfort and structural durability requirements for all TAYLKOMB parts. These requirements are **mandatory** — no part revision should be approved without meeting them.

---

## 2. Tooth Engineering (TC-001, TC-002, TC-003)

### 2.1 Root Fillets (CRITICAL)

Every tooth-to-spine junction must have a fillet radius to prevent stress-concentration fracture.

| Part | Min Root Fillet | Current Status |
|------|----------------|----------------|
| TC-001 Main Comb (fine teeth) | ≥0.50 mm | **NOT SPECIFIED — must add** |
| TC-002 Narrow Comb | ≥0.50 mm | **NOT SPECIFIED — must add** |
| TC-003 Wide Comb | ≥0.75 mm (wider spacing allows larger fillet) | **NOT SPECIFIED — must add** |

### 2.2 Tooth Taper (CRITICAL)

Teeth must be wider at the root and narrower at the tip to resist lateral bending.

| Part | Root Width | Tip Width | Max Aspect Ratio |
|------|-----------|----------|------------------|
| TC-001 | ≥0.80 mm | ≥0.50 mm | ≤25:1 |
| TC-002 | ≥0.90 mm | ≥0.60 mm | ≤22:1 |
| TC-003 | ≥1.20 mm | ≥0.80 mm | ≤20:1 |

### 2.3 Tooth Draft

All tooth sidewalls: ≥1.5° draft angle for injection mold demolding.

### 2.4 Crown Profile

| Feature | Requirement |
|---------|-------------|
| Crown peak tips | ≥1.5 mm fillet radius on ALL peaks |
| Crown spine thickness | ≥4.0 mm at peak centers |
| Crown valley fillets | ≥1.0 mm at all internal valleys |
| Crown valley mold venting | Required at deepest points |

## 3. Handle Ergonomics (TH-001, TH-002, TH-003)

### 3.1 Minimum Grip Dimensions

| Part | Minimum Cross-Section | Current | Change Required |
|------|----------------------|---------|-----------------|
| TH-001 Double Handle | 8.0 mm × 16.0 mm at fork base → OK | 8.0 × 27.2 mm | None |
| TH-002 Flat Handle | **≥3.5 mm thick** in grip zone | 2.54 mm | **Increase by +1.0 mm** |
| TH-003 Round Handle | **≥4.5 mm diameter** at narrowest grip | 3.11 mm | **Increase by +1.4 mm** |

### 3.2 Grip Texture Specification

| Part | Zone | Treatment | Depth |
|------|------|-----------|-------|
| TH-001 | 30–60 mm from receiver end | Fine diamond knurling (0.3 mm pitch) | 0.10–0.15 mm |
| TH-002 | Full body (both faces) | Matte bead-blast (Ra 1.6–3.2 μm) | Surface |
| TH-003 | 40–80 mm from receiver end | Circumferential grooves (0.5 mm pitch, 0.08 mm deep) | 0.08 mm |

**Optional upgrade:** Silicone overmold grip band on TH-002 for premium tier.

### 3.3 Edge Breaks

ALL external edges on ALL stainless handles must be broken:
- Working edges (spatula tip on TH-002, prong tips on TH-001, point on TH-003): ≥0.15 mm radius
- Non-working edges: ≥0.30 mm radius or 0.3 mm × 45° chamfer
- **No sharp edges anywhere on any handle surface that contacts the stylist's hand**

### 3.4 Transition Zones

| Part | Receiver → Body Transition | Min Taper Length | Min Fillet |
|------|---------------------------|-----------------|------------|
| TC-002 | 8×16 mm → 8×4 mm spine | 10 mm | 2.0 mm |
| TC-003 | 8×16 mm → 8×4 mm spine | 10 mm | 2.0 mm |
| TH-001 | 8×16 mm → fork spread | 15 mm | 2.0 mm |
| TH-002 | 8×16 mm → 7.64×3.5 mm | 12 mm | 1.5 mm |
| TH-003 | 8×16 mm → ∅4.5 mm | 15 mm | 2.0 mm (conical) |

### 3.5 Fork Junction Reinforcement (TH-001)

| Feature | Requirement |
|---------|-------------|
| Junction fillet radius | ≥3.0 mm on ALL faces (inner and outer) |
| Junction cross-section | ≥4.0 mm thick at thinnest point of split |
| Material continuity | No sharp internal corners within 5 mm of split point |

## 4. Button Ergonomics

### 4.1 Placement

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|-------|
| Side | +X face (consistent across all 5 attachments) |
| Z position | 35 mm from Datum C (opening face) |
| Proud from surface | 0–0.5 mm (flush to slightly proud) |
| Recess surround | ≥0.3 mm shoulder ring to prevent accidental press |
| Access | Reachable with thumb without repositioning grip |

### 4.2 Feel

| Parameter | Target |
|-----------|--------|
| Travel | 0.8–1.2 mm |
| Force | 5–8 N |
| Feedback | Tactile click at release point |
| Return | Spring returns button within 50 ms |

## 5. Structural Durability

### 5.1 Connection Joint

| Test | Target |
|------|--------|
| Lateral deflection (5 N at comb tip) | ≤1.0 mm at joint |
| Bending fatigue (2 N cyclic, 10K cycles) | No visible wear |
| Drop (1 m onto concrete, 6 orientations) | No separation |
| Torque resistance (0.5 Nm twist) | No rotation |

### 5.2 Cycle Life

| Milestone | Pull-Out Target |
|-----------|----------------|
| 0 (new) | ≥20 N |
| 1,000 | ≥18 N (90%) |
| 5,000 | ≥16 N (80%) |
| 10,000 | ≥15 N (75%) |

### 5.3 Environmental

| Test | Condition | Pass |
|------|-----------|------|
| Heat resistance | 200°C radiant, 30 min | No warping, lock functional |
| Chemical | Alcohol, acetone, styling products, 24 hr soak | No degradation |
| UV | 200 hr UV-A exposure | No discoloration (carbon); no corrosion (steel) |
| Salt spray | 500 hr ASTM B117 (steel parts) | No visible corrosion |
| Glue adhesion | Apply/remove hair glue 50 cycles (steel handles) | Glue peels off cleanly |

## 6. Weight Targets

| Part | Target Weight | Notes |
|------|--------------|-------|
| TC-001 Main Comb | 15–25 g | Carbon composite, lightweight |
| TC-002 Narrow Comb | 12–20 g | Carbon composite |
| TC-003 Wide Comb | 10–18 g | Fewer teeth = less material |

| TH-002 Flat Handle | 25–40 g | Thin profile, lighter |
| TH-003 Round Handle | 20–35 g | Small cross-section |

**Balance rule:** Assembled CG within 40% of total length from grip center.
